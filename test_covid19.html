Types of COVID-19 Test

There are multiple types of COVID-19 Test which include:

  1. Rapid Antigen Test
    • Rapid Antigen Test is a antigen that detect foreign or viral protein that triggers immune response of the body.
    • Usually will collect nasal or throat swap sample
    • It takes up to an hour to produce the result
    • Accuracy: Moderate
  2. RT-PCR
    • RT-PCR also called as molecule test is a test to detect the presence of virus in the sample through laboratory test.
    • Usually will collect nasal or throat swap sample
    • It takes up to 3 days to produce the result
    • Accuracy: Most Accurate
  3. Antibody Test
    • Antibody Test is a Serology test that detect the presence of virus through drawn blood.
    • Will collect blood sample
    • It takes up to 3 days to produce the result
    • Accuracy: Mostly Accurate



Time to Get Tested

COVID-19 test can be done by when:

  • Having symptoms of COVID-19
  • Close Contact with COVID-19
  • Before and After Travelling
  • Before and after treating a cut or wound
  • After being ask by the healthcare professional or public health official



Precautions Before Getting COVID-19 Test

  • Do not drink water 10 minutes before the test.
  • Don not eat or drink 30 minutes before the test.
